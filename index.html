How we choose our picks

We don’t test beds in a lab or run timed trials. Instead, we build our recommendations from the ground up using three sources: long-term owner feedback on real-world durability (what flattens, what frays, what starts to smell), published reviews from trusted pet-expert outlets, and the reputation of each brand for standing behind their products. We look for patterns: which beds are returned most often, which covers actually survive repeated washing, and which foams hold their shape beyond the first six months. We also pay close attention to what actually breaks. Zippers fail. Foam turns to dust. Bolsters collapse. A good bed is one that avoids those common failure points, and our picks reflect that. We weight owner-reported longevity more heavily than marketing claims, and we update our top picks whenever enough new evidence comes in to shift the consensus.
