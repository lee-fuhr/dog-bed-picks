Finding a bed that treats your dog well without breaking the bank can feel like a puzzle. Budget doesn’t have to mean flimsy, plenty of well-made beds exist under a hundred dollars that offer real orthopedic support, washable covers, and thoughtful designs. The trick is knowing which features matter most for your pup’s sleeping style. When shopping, focus on three things: the filling (look for solid foam, not loose stuffing that flattens), the cover (machine-washable is non-negotiable), and the size (many budget beds run small, so measure your dog sprawled out). Avoid anything that feels like a beanbag, it’ll lose shape in weeks. The three beds below have held up well in real homes according to owner feedback and expert reviews.
